# RemoteIQ User Guide: PWA Monitoring Platform

## What is RemoteIQ?

RemoteIQ is a modern, high-performance monitoring platform designed to give you real-time visibility into your IoT devices and industrial equipment. Whether you are managing a fleet of engines, pump stations, or other critical infrastructure, RemoteIQ provides the data you need to ensure operational efficiency and minimize downtime.

### Key Capabilities
*   **Real-Time Monitoring**: View engine speed (RPM), operating hours, temperature, and oil pressure as it happens.
*   **Fuel Management**: Track fuel levels and rates to optimize replenishment schedules.
*   **Location Tracking**: Integrated GPS data tells you exactly where your equipment is located.
*   **Remote Control**: Start or stop engines directly from the app (on supported hardware).
*   **Custom Branding**: Optimized for multiple service brands, such as **Sage Rental Services**, ensuring a familiar experience for your specific workflow.

## What is a Progressive Web App (PWA)?

RemoteIQ is delivered as a **Progressive Web App (PWA)**. This means it is a website that "progresses" into a full app experience once you save it to your home screen.

### Benefits vs. Native Apps
1.  **Instant Access**: No need to search the App Store or Google Play. Just visit the URL and you're ready to go.
2.  **Always Up-to-Date**: You never have to manually download an "update." The app refreshes itself every time you open it.
3.  **Low Storage**: PWAs take up significantly less space on your phone than traditional apps.
4.  **Cross-Platform**: The same app works perfectly on iPhone, Android, and Desktop.

### A Native App Experience
Despite being web-based, RemoteIQ feels exactly like a native app:
*   **Home Screen Icon**: Launch it directly from your home screen just like Instagram or Maps.
*   **Full Screen**: Once installed, the browser address bar and navigation buttons disappear, giving you a full-screen experience.
*   **Speed**: Built-in caching ensures the app loads instantly, even on slower connections.

## How to Add RemoteIQ to Your Home Screen

Follow these simple steps to install RemoteIQ on your device.

### For iPhone (Safari)
1.  **Open Safari** and navigate to your RemoteIQ URL.
2.  **Sign In**: Enter your credentials to access your dashboard.
3.  **Tap Share**: Tap the "Share" icon at the bottom of the screen (the square with an arrow pointing up).
4.  **Add to Home Screen**: Scroll down the menu and tap **"Add to Home Screen."**
5.  **Confirm**: Tap **"Add"** in the top right corner.

![Installation Step](file:///Users/charleschristie/.gemini/antigravity/brain/dd1adad6-1162-4f22-8ecd-a17b2f1ceefd/uploaded_media_3_1770066494542.png)
*Locate the Share button at the bottom of your Safari browser.*

### For Android (Chrome)
1.  **Open Chrome** and navigate to your RemoteIQ URL.
2.  **Tap Menu**: Tap the three vertical dots (menu) in the top right corner.
3.  **Install App**: Tap **"Install app"** or **"Add to Home screen."**
4.  **Confirm**: Follow the on-screen prompts to finish.
